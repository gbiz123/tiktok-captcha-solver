TikTok Captcha Solver

Instructions

  • Sign up on our website for a free trial
  • Launch Chrome with the --disable-web-security flag enabled. This is a necessary step to avoid CORS errors that prevent images from being downloaded. Click here to read more.
  • Log in to your account
  • Copy your key while logged in to the dashboard
  • The browser tab must be open to TikTok to enter your key
  • Enter your API key below and press "submit"

After entering your key, all TikTok captchas will be solved automatically. The key will remain stored in your browser, and you only need to enter it once.
